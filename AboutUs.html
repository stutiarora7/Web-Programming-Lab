About Windowshop.com


Windowshop.com is India's first impuslive shopper friendly website. This website helps users to buy clothes that are right for them, but also sets a limit to the number of products that can be bought, so as to curb incessent expenditure for impulsive buyers. There are various styles, varities and choices for users to choose from, making it an ideal place for someone who loves to shop (but on a budget!)

The store has launched many innovative features to ensure that users get an immersive buyer experience before ordering products that they like. This is a one-of-a-kind website that aims at giving customer satisfaction, as well as maintainance of budget for the users. There is an array of brands that the website deals with, making it a one-stop shopping location for products based on categories - for men, women and kids and also on the basis of brands.
